[中国向塔利班提出以公路换和平](https://cn.ft.com/story/001089327?full=y)
------

<div></div><div class="story-lead">据悉在北京举行的会谈中，中方提出对阿富汗的能源和基础设施项目进行可观投资，前提是塔利班在美军撤离后维护和平。</div><div class=" story-image image"><img src="https://thumbor.ftacademy.cn/unsafe/1340x754/https://thumbor.ftacademy.cn/unsafe/picture/0/000093180_piclink.jpg"></div><div class="story-body"><div
