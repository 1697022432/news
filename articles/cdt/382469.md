[小晖原创 | 呦呦鹿鸣已经失踪两个月了](https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/666106.html)
------

<p>作者：小晖</p><p>今天，我想为你们大家写一个人，他叫呦呦鹿鸣，他已经失踪两个月零五天了。</p><p>这是他的最后一篇文章，3月11日更新的，之后再无更新，已经整整两个月了。</p><p><noscript><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/05/image-1621312165241.png" alt="file"></noscript><img class="lazyload" src="data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20210%20140%22%3E%3C/svg%3E" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/05/image-1621312165241.png" alt="file"></p><p>他的最后一条微博也停止在3月8日那一天。</p><p><noscript><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/05/image-1621312183978.png" alt="file"></noscript><img class="lazyload" src="data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20210%20140%22%3E%3C/svg%3E" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/05/image-1621312183978.png" alt="file"></p><p>呦呦鹿鸣的真名叫黄志杰，曾是新华社《瞭望东方周刊》主笔，《网络传播》执行主编。</p><p>他跟其他媒体人不太一样的地方是，他写的基本上都是小人物生活状态，这些都是他写过的小人物。</p><p><noscript><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/05/image-1621312192091.png" alt="file"></noscript><img class="lazyload" src="data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20210%20140%22%3E%3C/svg%3E" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/05/image-1621312192091.png" alt="file"></p><p>我个人认为，从呦呦鹿鸣写的这些小人物就可以看出来，他是一个有温度的作者，他会写到社会的方方面面，比如山东过年磕头这事，他觉得不合理，于是就会写出来。</p><p>但也恰好是因为山东磕头这事，呦呦鹿鸣捅了马蜂窝了，很多人都在威胁他，让他不得不报案。</p><p><noscript><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/05/image-1621312210136.png" alt="file"></noscript><img class="lazyload" src="data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20210%20140%22%3E%3C/svg%3E" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/05/image-1621312210136.png" alt="file"></p><p>这是其中威胁他的一部分人名单。</p><p><noscript><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/05/image-1621312221982.png" alt="file"></noscript><img class="lazyload" src="data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20210%20140%22%3E%3C/svg%3E" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/05/image-1621312221982.png" alt="file"></p><p><noscript><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/05/image-1621312231486.png" alt="file"></noscript><img class="lazyload" src="data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20210%20140%22%3E%3C/svg%3E" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/05/image-1621312231486.png" alt="file"></p><p>其中不乏刘继兴这样的百万粉丝大V。</p><p><noscript><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/05/image-1621312242033.png" alt="file"></noscript><img class="lazyload" src="data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20210%20140%22%3E%3C/svg%3E" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/05/image-1621312242033.png" alt="file"></p><p>很多人过年期间，都在短信辱骂威胁呦呦鹿鸣，这是其中的一部分。</p><p><noscript><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/05/image-1621312253333.png" alt="file"></noscript><img class="lazyload" src="data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20210%20140%22%3E%3C/svg%3E" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/05/image-1621312253333.png" alt="file"></p><p>看到这样的短信，让人无法不担心他。</p><p><noscript><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/05/image-1621312262693.png" alt="file"></noscript><img class="lazyload" src="data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20210%20140%22%3E%3C/svg%3E" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/05/image-1621312262693.png" alt="file"></p><p>这是打进来的骚扰电话，让他不得安宁。</p><p><noscript><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/05/image-1621312272383.png" alt="file"></noscript><img class="lazyload" src="data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20210%20140%22%3E%3C/svg%3E" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/05/image-1621312272383.png" alt="file"></p><p>甚至有人说要拿100万去收拾呦呦鹿鸣。</p><p><noscript><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/05/image-1621312280793.png" alt="file"></noscript><img class="lazyload" src="data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20210%20140%22%3E%3C/svg%3E" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/05/image-1621312280793.png" alt="file"></p><p><noscript><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/05/image-1621312289684.png" alt="file"></noscript><img class="lazyload" src="data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20210%20140%22%3E%3C/svg%3E" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/05/image-1621312289684.png" alt="file"></p><p>呦呦鹿鸣之前就因为文章被人威胁过，只不过这一次威胁他的人更多。</p><p><noscript><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/05/image-1621312450234.png" alt="file"></noscript><img class="lazyload" src="data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20210%20140%22%3E%3C/svg%3E" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/05/image-1621312450234.png" alt="file"></p><p>呦呦鹿鸣选择报案的原因很简单，这一次不少人威胁到了他的家人，他害怕了。</p><p><noscript><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/05/image-1621312460758.png" alt="file"></noscript><img class="lazyload" src="data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20210%20140%22%3E%3C/svg%3E" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/05/image-1621312460758.png" alt="file"></p><p>尽管有7000名网友给他打赏，但呦呦鹿鸣还是害怕了，坚持更新了半个月后，再也坚持不下去了。</p><p><noscript><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/05/image-1621312475529.png" alt="file"></noscript><img class="lazyload" src="data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20210%20140%22%3E%3C/svg%3E" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/05/image-1621312475529.png" alt="file"></p><p>思前想后之后，呦呦鹿鸣就这样消失了，而且还消失了两个月，不管他的粉丝怎么呼唤他，他都没回来。</p><p>现在他的粉丝们，只能看着他过往的文字以作慰藉，比如他写三农和农民的。</p><p><noscript><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/05/image-1621312485779.png" alt="file"></noscript><img class="lazyload" src="data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20210%20140%22%3E%3C/svg%3E" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/05/image-1621312485779.png" alt="file"></p><p>比如他写贵州毕节女教师周琴被强奸的。</p><p><noscript><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/05/image-1621312493828.png" alt="file"></noscript><img class="lazyload" src="data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20210%20140%22%3E%3C/svg%3E" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/05/image-1621312493828.png" alt="file"></p><p><noscript><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/05/image-1621312502605.png" alt="file"></noscript><img class="lazyload" src="data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20210%20140%22%3E%3C/svg%3E" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/05/image-1621312502605.png" alt="file"></p><p>比如他写下的这个理解：何为中国，又何以为中国？</p><p><noscript><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/05/image-1621312509731.png" alt="file"></noscript><img class="lazyload" src="data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20210%20140%22%3E%3C/svg%3E" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/05/image-1621312509731.png" alt="file"></p><p>比如他认为，有些底线，不管用什么样的理由都是不能突破的。</p><p><noscript><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/05/image-1621312520072.png" alt="file"></noscript><img class="lazyload" src="data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20210%20140%22%3E%3C/svg%3E" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/05/image-1621312520072.png" alt="file"></p><p>比如，他认为教育才是扶贫的最好手段。</p><p><noscript><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/05/image-1621312528201.png" alt="file"></noscript><img class="lazyload" src="data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20210%20140%22%3E%3C/svg%3E" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/05/image-1621312528201.png" alt="file"></p><p>也因为这样有温度，有情怀的话语，呦呦鹿鸣的读者很信任他，有12000人为他发起的公益活动捐款。</p><p><noscript><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/05/image-1621312542077.png" alt="file"></noscript><img class="lazyload" src="data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20210%20140%22%3E%3C/svg%3E" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/05/image-1621312542077.png" alt="file"></p><p>通过上面这些文字，以及读者对他的信任，我可以说，呦呦鹿鸣是一个有良知，也有情怀的自媒体作者，因为他说的都是人话，而且都是在为底层民众说话，我们就需要这样的作者，也欣喜有这样的作者。</p><p>可是没想到的是，呦呦鹿鸣这样的讲人话的作者，居然会被某些人视为寇仇，派人威胁他骚扰他，现在如他们所愿了，呦呦鹿鸣已经两个月没说话了，难道这就是讲人话作者的必然下场么？</p><p>他的读者有两个月没见着他了，应该很想念他吧？</p><p>我也很想念呦呦鹿鸣，他的文字风格，他的理念真的影响到了我，特别是说人话这一点，更是成为我对自己的一个要求。</p><p>可是这么好的作者，居然就这样消失了，整整两个月一点消息都没有，他还好吗？我想知道，他的读者们也想知道。</p><div
