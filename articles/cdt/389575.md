[【真理部】2020美国大选](https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/2020/11/%e3%80%90%e7%9c%9f%e7%90%86%e9%83%a8%e3%80%912020%e7%be%8e%e5%9b%bd%e5%a4%a7%e9%80%89/)
------
