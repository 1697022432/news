[小晖有思｜李承鹏把高晓松的皮给扒了](https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/668028.html)
------

<p>这两天，又有一对老朋友绝交了，他们就是高晓松和李承鹏。</p><p>事情的起因并不复杂，高晓松发了一条微博，李承鹏看不下去了，于是这样揶揄他。</p><p><noscript><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/07/post-668028-60e6d1dd4ea27.png" alt="image"></noscript><img class="lazyload" src="data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20210%20140%22%3E%3C/svg%3E" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/07/post-668028-60e6d1dd4ea27.png" alt="image"></p><p>看来这两人是真的撕破脸了，大眼又抖露出高晓松过往的不少料。十年前高晓松问年轻人为什么一定要买房，李承鹏嘲讽他站着说话不腰痛，在美国买了大宅子的他，没权力嘲笑年轻人。</p><p><noscript><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/07/post-668028-60e6d1dd9f86b.png" alt="image"></noscript><img class="lazyload" src="data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20210%20140%22%3E%3C/svg%3E" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/07/post-668028-60e6d1dd9f86b.png" alt="image"></p><p>九年前，高晓松又批评了年轻人，李承鹏又嘲讽了一次，让他不要拿着美国绿卡说这样的话。</p><p><noscript><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/07/post-668028-60e6d1dde9bf6.png" alt="image"></noscript><img class="lazyload" src="data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20210%20140%22%3E%3C/svg%3E" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/07/post-668028-60e6d1dde9bf6.png" alt="image"></p><p>李承鹏在这里还爆出一个猛料，高晓松的晓说是占用了清华女博士助手帮忙写的稿件，在这个基础上才有了大名鼎鼎的晓说。</p><p><noscript><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/07/post-668028-60e6d1de33858.png" alt="image"></noscript><img class="lazyload" src="data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20210%20140%22%3E%3C/svg%3E" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/07/post-668028-60e6d1de33858.png" alt="image"></p><p>在最后，李承鹏说了一句很毒舌的话，依然不改当年的本色。</p><p><noscript><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/07/post-668028-60e6d1de7a762.png" alt="image"></noscript><img class="lazyload" src="data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20210%20140%22%3E%3C/svg%3E" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/07/post-668028-60e6d1de7a762.png" alt="image"></p><p>从最后这句话里可以看出来，这对老朋友是真的绝交了，要不然也不会说出这么绝情的话来。</p><p>看到这一幕，不由让人感叹，时光啊时光，到底要让多少知交好友变成陌路人，遥想2010年时，高晓松和李承鹏还能相谈甚欢。</p><p><noscript><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/07/post-668028-60e6d1deae98d." alt="image"></noscript><img class="lazyload" src="data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20210%20140%22%3E%3C/svg%3E" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/07/post-668028-60e6d1deae98d." alt="image"></p><p>11年的时候，高晓松还说要帮李承鹏卖书。</p><p><noscript><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/07/post-668028-60e6d1df04c07.png" alt="image"></noscript><img class="lazyload" src="data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20210%20140%22%3E%3C/svg%3E" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/07/post-668028-60e6d1df04c07.png" alt="image"></p><p>在11年时，高晓松还称自己与李承鹏永远是好哥们。</p><p><noscript><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/07/post-668028-60e6d1df4b113.png" alt="image"></noscript><img class="lazyload" src="data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20210%20140%22%3E%3C/svg%3E" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/07/post-668028-60e6d1df4b113.png" alt="image"></p><p>然而时隔几个月后，两人第一次闹翻，从这里开始，两人渐行渐远。</p><p><noscript><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/07/post-668028-60e6d1df99e94.png" alt="image"></noscript><img class="lazyload" src="data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20210%20140%22%3E%3C/svg%3E" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/07/post-668028-60e6d1df99e94.png" alt="image"></p><p>这是双方的第二轮交锋。</p><p><noscript><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/07/post-668028-60e6d1dfcb14e." alt="image"></noscript><img class="lazyload" src="data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20210%20140%22%3E%3C/svg%3E" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/07/post-668028-60e6d1dfcb14e." alt="image"></p><p>这是北京晨报当年对这件事的报道。</p><p><noscript><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/07/post-668028-60e6d1e00a37f." alt="image"></noscript><img class="lazyload" src="data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20210%20140%22%3E%3C/svg%3E" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/07/post-668028-60e6d1e00a37f." alt="image"></p><p><noscript><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/07/post-668028-60e6d1e03221b.png" alt="image"></noscript><img class="lazyload" src="data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20210%20140%22%3E%3C/svg%3E" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/07/post-668028-60e6d1e03221b.png" alt="image"></p><p>就这样，当年的一对好友就这样渐行渐远，而且价值观差异越来越大，看着他们的同框照片，只能让人感慨。</p><p><noscript><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/07/post-668028-60e6d1e061148." alt="image"></noscript><img class="lazyload" src="data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20210%20140%22%3E%3C/svg%3E" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/07/post-668028-60e6d1e061148." alt="image"></p><p>高是知道装不知道，申请为正能量；李是知道装不了不知道，被判为负能量；高晓松现在到处晃悠挣钱，是现代文人的成功代表，老了会后悔；李承鹏则会一直闭关，老了就老了，但至少能求得个心安。</p><p>就这样，又一对名人朋友彻底闹翻，要老死不相往来了。</p><div class="addtoany_share_save_container
